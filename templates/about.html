I am Deepanshu Prasad, first year student of B.Tech degree in Electrical Engineering at S V National Institute of Technology, Surat. This is my project "C$50 Finance".

This project is part of the Web Track under the course CS50. This is actually a website which is similar to any other Online Stock Trading site, with difference here being that the money you invest is not real. Building this website helped in improving my knowledge of several programming languages, like:


HTML

Cascading Style Sheet (CSS)

JavaScript

SQL

Python

There are so many extra features (personal touches) included in this website other than the original features, that were required. We will look at both of them :

Original Features
You can buy shares of stocks with the cash you have in your wallet, in real time.
You will have your own account form which you can login and logout into the web app. All of the data will be saved in database and will be unique to you.
Shares bought can be sold anytime you would like to, as per the trend at that time.
Registaration will be done and all your data will be saved securely on our database with your unique account.
You can search for the current price and trend of the desired stock. Some reference websites are also provided to check for the symbol of the company you want to get.
All of your bought and sold history will be stored and can be viewed at a single place.
Personal Touch
See the amount of cash you are left with and money you can get by selling all the shares you own. Also add money to the wallet if you need.
User can change their username and password from the Profile section and can delete their account permanently.
Go to the wallet section and there you can take loan of max. $1,500 at a time. All your loan history will be saved.
You can buy more or sell some of the shares of any stock you own, directly from the owned page, without entering the symbol as text value.
Constraints have been added to password. It should be of more than 8 characters and must contain a number, and not anything else than alphabet and number.
There is a significant change in the User Interface. Instead of using Bootstrap, I have written all the CSS code myself.
Apology page has been changed as previous one operated on different layout. You can view the page by switching off the internet and re-loading.
Length of username should maximum be 10 and minimum 4 charcters . Username should not contain anything else than alphabets and numbers. Also it should be unique. Two users cannot have same username.
All new Home Page is introduced where you will get to see a welcome message and any three of your owned stocks.
With price and name you will also be able to see the change in price of your stocks.
